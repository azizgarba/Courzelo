Incentives Page

see your Incentives

valid votes

5 votes every 24h

If a professor reaches 5 valid votes, they can't vote for 24 hours.

Accumulating Incentive Votes

.

Each valid vote contributes to the accumulation of incentive votes for the professor.

getting the vote bonus

voting for a month(150 votes) every day 5 votes

When the total number of incentive votes reaches 150, the professor receives a vote bonus.

the deliberation of the vote bonus

.

A vote bonus is recorded with the current date and added to the bonus table. Bonuses are granted based on the professor's payment date. If the bonus date falls before the payment date in the current month, the bonus is awarded this month, otherwise it's postponed to the next month.

Deleteing vote

When a vote is removed we update the incentive vote count, and decreasing it if it was different from 0.

Explanation Incentive

Chat room

Professors can enter a chat room with students to clarify questions posed by the students and explain them

getting the explanation bonus

the professor can get one inventive only every 24h

The student can approve the best explanation, which awards an Explanation Incentive (the type of incentive) to the corresponding professor. This incentive is also recorded in the Incentives table.

Badge

How to get a badge

.

The number of vote bonuses for badges (nbPrimeVoteForBadge) is incremented by 1 each time the professor receives a vote bonus, in two cases

The two Conditions

.

When the counter was at 0 or If the last vote incentive obtained was from the preceding month of the new vote incentive obtained, then the counter is incremented by 1. However, it resets to 0

getting the badge

.

If nbPrimeVoteForBadge reaches 5 (meaning the professor has received 5 vote bonuses per day for 5 consecutive months), a check is performed to see if there are at least 5 explanation incentives in the same period from the IncentivesVote table. If so, the professor receives a reward badge, and nbPrimeVoteForBadge is reset to 0. Otherwise, nbPrimeVoteForBadge is simply reset to 0 without awarding a badge.
